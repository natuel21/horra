Food Safety Policy

Horra Food Complex PLC is strongly committed to produce and provide safe high quality Wheat Flour, Biscuit and pasta /Macaroni/ products that fulfill applicable international, national and mutually agreed Food Safety Standards based on /ISO 22000:2005/ regulatory and statutory requirements to the highest satisfaction of its customers.

with strong commitiment and participation of the management and employees at all levels. the company implement, maintain and continually improve food safety managementsystem; train and aware every employee ensure allocation and effective utilization of resources establish effective communication arrangement prepare and respond to emergency situaions and accidents and ensure the continuing suitablity adequacy and effectiveness of the system.

The policy is communicated, understood, implemented, reviewed updated and maintained at all levels of all organization
