History

Horra Foods is the largest food industy in ethiopia that produces Wheat flour ,Pasta Macaroni and Biscuits.Completed in 2012 GC This complex houses a pasta and hard cookies production plants, plus ancillary facilities, site works and utilities distribution over a site of circa 7000m2 south-west of Addis Ababa.The project took 36 months to complete.
